Place new requests:

To request materials from Tufts Archival Research Center or Tisch Rare Books collection, please follow the instructions below.

Archives and Manuscripts:

Discover and request materials at Archives@Tufts and the Tufts Digital Library. Vist Navigating your TASCR Account for more information.

Rare Books and other published materials:

Request through JumboSearch.

Ritter Collection (Tufts University's Special Collection in Music)

Some Ritter Collection materials are cataloged in JumboSearch, and may be requested through the catalog. If you cannot find a listing for the book you are looking for, please contact us at archives@tufts.edu.

Order Reproductions:

To order reproductions of archival materials from Tufts Archival Research Center, search Archives@Tufts, the Tufts Digital Library or JumboSearch. Vist Navigating your TASCR Account for more information.
